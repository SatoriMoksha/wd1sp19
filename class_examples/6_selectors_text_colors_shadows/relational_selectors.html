CSS Relational Selectors

Descendent Selector

HTML

<h2>First Heading</h2>
<article>
  <h2>Heading, child of article</h2>
  <p>Some Text</p>
  <div>
    <h2>Heading, child of div, descendent of article</h2>
    <p>Some Text</p>
  </div>
</article>

CSS

article h2 {
  background: yellow;
}

Output:

First Heading

Heading, child of article

Some Text

Heading, child of div, descendent of article

Some Text

Child Selector

<h2>First Heading</h2>

Heading, child of div, descendent of article

Some Text

General Sibling Selector

HTML

<p>Outside Section</p>
<section>
  <p>Child of Section</p>
  <h2>Child of Section</h2>
  <p>Child of Section</p>
  <div>
    <p>Child of Div</p>
  </div>
  <p>Child of Section</p>
</section>

CSS

h2~p {
  background: yellow;
}

Output:

Outside Section

Child of Section

Child of Section

Child of Section

Child of Div

Child of Section
